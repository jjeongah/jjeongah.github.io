AR Music Note

March 2020 - June 2020
App guiding K-pop idol fans to concert venues, enhancing their journey with an enjoyable and memorable experience

[Project Description]
As part of an industry-university cooperation project with SK Planet, our team developed an app aimed at promoting the 2020 Busan One Asia Festival. 'AR Music Note' is an augmented reality (AR) location-based service designed to guide users to the event venue and offer an enjoyable rhythm game experience.
[User Secnario]
  • When a user launches the app at Haeundae Station and the street is recognized, an album image of K-pop idols, presented as an object, is displayed on the screen in real-time alignment with the user's location.
  • Subsequently, upon the user's interaction with the object, the game commences, featuring AR notes descending to the rhythm of the songs in the album.
  • The game concludes as the user arrives at the venue, with scores being tallied.
  • At the event booth, prizes, such as idol photo cards and albums, are awarded to users based on their scores.
