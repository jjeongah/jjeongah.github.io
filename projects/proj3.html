Open Domain Question Answering(ODQA)

November 19, 2022 - January 5, 2023
Model that answers questions based on world knowledge when no paragraph is given

[Task Description]
- ODQA is an NLP task that answers questions based on world knowledge when no paragraph is given.
- This model consists of a "retriever" step that finds relevant documents and a "reader" step that finds appropriate answers among those documents.
[Dataset]
For the MRC (Machine Reading Comprehension) data, our team utilized the datasets library provided by HuggingFace.
