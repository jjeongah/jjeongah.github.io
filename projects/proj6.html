Wi-Fi indoor location recognition app

July 2020 - December 2020
Service that provides customized advertisements or benefits based on the real-time location of customers in indoor multiplex

System diagram

[Project Description]
As part of an industry-university cooperation project with SK Planet, our team have developed a service that utilizes WiFi fingerprinting techniques to deliver personalized advertisements and benefits to customers in real-time, within indoor multi-use facilities like department stores and shopping malls.
[User Secnario]
The service operates through a two-step scenario:
  • First, the service manager collects WiFi signals, from which a location fingerprints database is constructed, and the classifier is trained.
  • Subsequently, the user's app determines the user's indoor location zone and delivers customized advertisements tailored to that specific zone.
